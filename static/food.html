Food Recommendations

To my dear reader, feel free to try as many of the following places as you would like, it took me many enjoyable trial and error to compile this wonderful list.


Montreal

Korean/Japanese/Chinese/Thai Food

  • Nos The - Curry Tonkatsu. My go to place when meeting friends in downtown area. It is a very cozy shop with delicious food and good options for bubble tea.
  • Nouilles de Lanzhou - Beef noodles with thick noodles. Better than most of the popular choices I tried in Toronto
  • Mon ami - Fried chicken
  • Vua - Saigon Banh Mi. They are 3-4$ each and the balance of ingredients and sauce is awesome.
  • Sam Cha - Buddae Jiggae

Vegetarian Food

  • Aux Vivres - All their options include 2-3 type of food and it is impossible to go wrong with any.
  • Pushap - Thali. Great value for your money since they are 4-5$ for each plate. The samosas are huge and 5 for 2 bucks.

If you love potatoes

  • La Belle Province - Frite Sauce. It is a poutine no cheese and is 30% cheaper. La belle province offers some of the best poutine in the city.
  • La Banquise - More fancy poutine. Extremely good value for the price you are paying and their frites have an unique sweet taste.
  • Allo Mon Coco - Generous serving of brunch food, their potatoes are the right mix of mashed and fried.

Desserts

  • St - Viateur or Fairmont Bagels
  • C Chocolat - Any mason jar, especially the Matcha Matcha Man
  • O Piano or Cafe Momo - Mango Bingsoo
  • Rockaberry - apple crumble
  • La Diperie - Best bang for the buck are the small cones. They dip the ice cream in warm chocolate syrup
  • Les glaciers - Amazing flavourful cupcakes, especially the red velvet cupcake.
  • Point G - On the higher end of my budget, but their pistachio macarons are amazing.

San Francisco

Peruvian/Lousiana Food

  • Limon - Quarter Dark Rotisserie Chicken, best Peruvian food
  • HardKnox Cafe - Homemade Corn Muffins, so cheap too

Korean/Japanese/Chinese/Thai Food

  • China North Dumpling - Any dumpling! They are huge and so yummy
  • Boba Guys - Strawberry Latte Matcha

Take Out/ Lunch Break

  • Miss Tomato - Chicken Masala Sandwich for 8$
  • Dabba - Large bowl of Mexican/Jamaican/Indian Fusion
