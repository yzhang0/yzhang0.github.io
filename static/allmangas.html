All about the Mangas


    After a difficult term at the university of Waterloo, I was afraid to code any middle size project by myself. The brutal CS courses made me want to flee any kind of intensive keyboard interaction. As such, I leeched on FLowing Data for months before trying my hand at data visualization... coding suddenly became fun again.

    I was fortunate enough to have a fellow Data Vis enthusiast as manager at IBM, who encouraged me to keep learning about this amazing library. In winter 2016, I created two projects with D3: a simple scatterplot (inspired by Hans Rosling's demographic chart) and a dynamic bubble chart (following an amazing tutorial by Jim Vallandigha).

    As an avid manga reader I was surprised by the lack of reliable data visualization about this form of literature. If you want to know which mangas are worth your time and money, don't forget to click on these links!
